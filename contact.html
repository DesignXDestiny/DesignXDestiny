How Services are Executed

Think it is a good fit? Is there a project you would like me to help you with? This is how my services work. This process applies to traditional art and web or coding projects.

Preparation

First, we decide if we have something to talk about. Then, we schedule a time/ place to talk about it. Exchange contact information, I will look at what you already have to work with, like an existing website, photos, assets, copy. I will look into your competitors at this point (Market Research).

Consultation

At our consultation, I want you to tell me what your dreams are. What are your Goals and Aspirations? Why are is that what you want to do or see happen? How did you become inspired? Feel as though you can speak to me freely. Tell me which plans and features your heart is set on. Tell me your smaller ideas, even if you're not sure how to make them work. Let it all out.

Designing

This is the creative stage. This is important because the less mistakes there will be later. Have you heard the saying, "Measure twice, cut once"? The same concept applies here. I will make a preliminary example or prototype, mock-up, thumbnail sketch, whichever is easiest to communicate with for your project. To make sure we are on the same page before continuing to build something. It is like looking up the destination on Google maps before driving there.

Medium Output
Web / Code Static version, colored pencil, .jpeg mock up
Graphic Design, Logo, Shirts, Cups, Printable, Screen-able, digital art Sketch, black and white line drawing, digital or traditional drawing
Traditional Art, Murals Thumbnails, Sketches, Compositions

Confirmation

If you're unsatisfied with what I made, we do it again. It is okay to go through a couple of drafts. When the plans look to you like they will produce something you like, we push forward. The more we communicate the more efficient this process becomes. Tell me what you like, and don't like.

Development

Now I / we put all the pieces of the puzzle together. Following what e talked about, with your vision in mind, we put pen to paper, key to code, brush to paint, pixel to web.

Deployment

Once the final version is approved by you, then we show everyone what we have created. Our Project is then uploaded, printed, and / or shipped.
